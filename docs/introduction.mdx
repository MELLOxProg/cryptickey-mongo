## Welcome to CrypticKey

CrypticKey is a sophisticated yet easy-to-use password manager that helps you keep your digital life secure. With a sleek interface and powerful features, CrypticKey ensures that your passwords are safe, organized, and accessible whenever you need them.

Our mission is to provide a seamless experience for managing your passwords without compromising on security. Whether you're an individual looking to protect your personal accounts or a team managing shared credentials, CrypticKey has you covered.

### Key Features

- **Secure Password Storage:** All your passwords are encrypted and stored securely in a MongoDB database.
- **Intuitive Interface:** A clean and modern user interface built with React and Tailwind CSS makes managing your passwords a breeze.
- **Quick Access:** Easily search, view, edit, and delete your saved passwords.
- **One-Click Copy:** Copy usernames and passwords to your clipboard with a single click.
- **Self-Hosted:** You have full control over your data by hosting CrypticKey on your own infrastructure.
- **RESTful API:** A well-documented API allows for easy integration and extension.

### Project Stack

- **Frontend:** React, Vite, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Deployment:** Vercel
